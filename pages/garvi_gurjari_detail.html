Project Information

Gujarat State Handloom & Handicrafts Development Corporation Ltd., (GSHHDC), an undertaking of Government of Gujarat was established in 1973 with the main objective of identification, revival, development of handicrafts and handlooms of Gujarat. The iOS application is an E-commerce app that provides a variety of products that users can buy online. Application also provides Cart facility in that users can add multiple products and see total billing.

  • Role: Developer
  • Responsibility: Development, Bug Fixes, Testing and Release
  • Tool: Xcode (10.0)
  • Programming Language: Swift (4.2), Objective-C
  • Architecture: MVC
  • Databse: SQL, CoreData, UserDefaults
  • API: REST
  • Notifications: APNS
  • Functionality: Push Notification, Payment SDK, E-commerce app
