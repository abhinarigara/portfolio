Project Information

Shree Maruti Courier Services is one of the Asia Pacific's leading courier services and an integrated express package distribution company with high reliability and satisfactory ratio. As we developed an iOS application with following features.

  • This application is dedicated for Channel Partner (CP) Manager
  • Manager can view data of Business statistics of their respective branch (e.g.: Total Collection, Total Booking Count, Delivery Status Count)
  • User can have list of clients of his/her branch with their detail
  • Shipment Tracking
  • User can view their profile

  • Role: Sr.Developer
  • Responsibility: Development, Bug Fixes, Testing and Release
  • Tool: Xcode (10.1)
  • Programming Language: Swift (4.2.4)
  • Architecture: MVC
  • Databse: SQL, UserDefaults
  • API: REST
  • Notifications: APNS
  • Functionality: Barcode Scanner, Get delivery status
