Vijay Home Services

Project Information

The iOS application is developed to constantly provide online services to customers. Services includes all types of Home based services like home Cleaning, Painting, Pest control, marble polish, Sanitization and many more. Using application customers can book a variety of services at their preferred time and place and also pay online via different modes of payments.

  • Role: Sr.Developer
  • Responsibility: Development, Bug Fixes, Testing and Release
  • Tool: Xcode (10.1)
  • Programming Language: Swift (4.2.4)
  • Architecture: MVC
  • Databse: SQL, UserDefaults
  • API: REST
  • Notifications: APNS
  • Functionality: Payment SDK, YouTube Videos, Book Services online, Push Notification
